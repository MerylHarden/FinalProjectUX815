STEWARD

A RISING TIDE LIFTS ALL BOATS

As a Black American designer, my work is informed by lived experience and cultural historical knowledge. The Steward logo draws from Adinkra symbols, a visual language from West Africa, specifically regions now known as Ghana and Côte d’Ivoire.

Building on this foundation, Steward translates these principles into an Electronic Health Record case management system designed around the realities of front-line mental healthcare work.

Steward is designed with the belief that when we focus on the needs of those who are dedicated to uplifting others, we strengthen society as a whole.
